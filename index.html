Navigate Page

Simply type in the name of any artist to the search box ("Queen", "Harry Styles", "Elton John") and click "GO" or hit your enter button! Select your desired artist to view their full discography, and hit the expand button to see every track on each respective album. Explore more artists with the Related Artists tab, clicking and viewing other artists' discographies. When you're ready for a new search, click the X at the top right and submit a new search to discover more!

About Us

We created this website to help people discover, learn, and expand their music horizons. As two music enthusiasts, we are constantly looking for new ways to grow our personal arsenals of music. We find that our website allows us to find new artists to listen to, and learn more about the artists we already listen to. We hope you are able to share this same experience!
